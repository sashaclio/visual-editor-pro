# Before / After Example

This example shows how `Visual Editor Pro` can be used to move from an original product-style image to a more refined editorial result.

## Example Overview

- **Subject**: Product / still life image placed among green leaves
- **Original image**: Natural capture with softer contrast and less defined focal emphasis
- **Edited result**: Stronger visual hierarchy, brighter greens, clearer product framing, and a more polished editorial feel

## What Changed

### Original Image

The original image has a natural, softer look. The product is visible, but the frame feels flatter and the visual hierarchy is less controlled.

### Edited Image

The edited result increases the brightness and presence of the surrounding greens, gives the gold packaging more visual definition, and creates a stronger center of attention around the product label. The overall effect feels more intentional and more aligned with an editorial still-life direction.

## Why This Example Matters

This pair demonstrates that the skill is not just descriptive. It can support a repeatable workflow for image diagnosis, retouching direction, and creative refinement.
